Steak

How to cook a steak.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Put oil in pan and heat on high
  2. Pat dry steak and season on both sides
  3. Put steak in pan and cook both sides 5min each
  4. Ad butter and spoon over steak
  5. Remove from heat and let rest for 5 min before slicing
