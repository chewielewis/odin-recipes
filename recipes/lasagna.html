Lasagna

An easy to make Lasagna recipe. Very quick to make and only a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Lasagnia Sheets
  • Beef Mince
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Onion
  • White Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water on the stove
  2. Chop onions
  3. Brown mince in a pan with a bit of oil
  4. Add onions
  5. Add pasta sauce
  6. Put lasagna sheets in boiling water for 3 mins
  7. Layer in baking dish, White sauce, then mince, then cheese, then sheets, repeat
  8. Bake for 1 hour
