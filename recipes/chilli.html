Chilli

How to cook Chilli.

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Can of Kidney Beans
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chilli Spices
  • Onion
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Put oil in pan and heat on high
  2. Chop onions
  3. Add onions to pan, cook till translucent
  4. Remove onions from pan and add mince
  5. Cook mince till browned, add onions back into pan
  6. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, spice mix, kidney beans
  7. Simmer for 30 mins
